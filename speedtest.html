Why Test Your Internet Speed?

Regular speed tests help you ensure you're getting the internet performance you're paying for. Here's why it matters:

  • Verify that your connection meets the speeds promised by your package
  • Identify potential issues with your network setup
  • Determine if you need to upgrade your package for better performance
  • Check if your router or equipment needs an upgrade

If your speed test results are consistently lower than expected, contact our support team for assistance.
